Welcome to The Medical Section!!


1.You can visit the Netmeds Website if you have Android phone you can download
it from Play Store if you have iPhone you download it from IOS app Store.
Now you can login if you alredy have an account or you can sign up to create an account.
You can follow the same steps for 1mg App Or PharmEasy App.


2.Now Sign Up using phone number and click on 'Get OTP' to verify your OTP.


3.Now Create your account by entering E-mail Id, First Name, Last Name, entering
your password, confirming your password and click on 'Verify OTP'.


4.Now Upload your prescriptions given by the Doctor and click on 'Continue'.


5.Check the number of quantity you need to buy and click on 'Proceed'.


6.Now enter Pin Code, City, State, Name, Address where the medicines need be delivered
and Phone number and click on 'Save Address'.


7.Now check your address and click on 'Confirm order'.


8.Congratulations you have Sucessfully placed your order!!
