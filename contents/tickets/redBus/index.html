Welcome to the Ticket Section!!


1.First you can visit the Website or if you have Android phone you can download it
from Google Play Store,if you have iPhone you download it from IOS app Store.
Now Enter the Source and Destination locations in the “From” and “To” tab
respectively. Enter the “Date of Journey” and hit the “Search” button.
Or You can follow the same step to book ticket through Goibibo App.


2.Select a bus of your choice from the list of buses that are displayed and check the
avalibilty of Seats and then click on "View Seats".


3.Choose your favourite seats, boarding, and dropping points and hit “Proceed to Book”.


4.Now fill the Passenger Details like Name, age, Gender, E-mail Id and Phone number
and click on "Proceed to Pay".


5.Now choose the mode of the Payments, you can pay through Debits/Credit Cards, UPI Id
Payments,and Netbanking. For Debit/Credit Cards payment, enter your Card Number,
Date of Expiray,CVV number and Name on your Card and click on 'Pay'.You will get an
OTP on your number, by entering the OTP, your payment will be proceeded and your
order will be sucessfully placed!!
