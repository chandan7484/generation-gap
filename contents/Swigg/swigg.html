Welcome to The Food Section!!


1.First you can visit the Swiggy website or if you have Android phone you can
download it from Play Store,if you have iPhone you download it from IOS app Store.
Now Open the Swiggy app or webpage and click on 'Locate Me' or 'Enter the Location'.
Or you can follow the same steps to order the food by Zomato App


2.Choose your favorite neighborhood restaurants and select the meal of your choice.


3.Now choose the meals of your choice and click on 'Add' to add items to your cart.


4.Now check the quantity of items in your cart and click on 'Check out'.


5.If you already have account, login into your account by using phone number,
If not then create your swiggy account by clicking on 'Sign Up'.


6.Now enter your Phone number, Name, E-Mail Id ,and password for your account
and then click on 'Continue'.


7.Check your delivery address click on 'Delivere Here' or if you want to change you
can change it by clicking on 'Add New'.


8.Now choose the mode of payment, you can pay through Wallets, Credit/ Debit
Cards, UPI ID payment, or by Net Banking. For Debit/Credit Cards payment, enter your
Card Number, Date of Expiray, CVV number and Name on your Card and click on 'Pay'.
You will get an OTP on your number, by entering the OTP, your payment will be
proceeded and your order will be sucessfully placed!!
